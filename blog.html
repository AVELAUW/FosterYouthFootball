This weeks win against Tyee!

Foster wins 28-21, August 15, 2024

Foster takes away the win

The game started off slow. Both teams couldn't seem to score any touchdowns in the 1st quarter. Suddenly, Foster ends up getting a pick six late in the second quarter with a minute left in the half. With only 40 seconds left Tyee had a chance to score a touchdown before the half, but Foster got a stop and took it to the half. Coming out of a slow first half, Tyee came out with an effortless score early in the second half to make it 7-7. Foster answers right back with another score to make the 14-7. Foster kicks the ball off to Tyee and they fumble the ball which gives Foster great field position to later score another touchdown making the score 21-7. After the first mistake on kickoff, Tyee makes up for it with a kick return touchdown at the end of the third quarter, 21-14. Foster takes the ball down the field, slowly shredding time, to score another touchdown making the score 28-14. Tyee did not give up with 2 minutes left in the game, scoring another empressive touchdown in the last mintute of the game, 28-21. Foster runs the rest of time off the clock and takes home the win, 28-21!
